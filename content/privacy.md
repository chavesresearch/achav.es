This site (achav.es) is the personal academic portfolio of Antonio Jesús Chaves García. It does not use accounts, does not run advertising, and does not include any contact or comment forms that collect visitor data. This page explains the little data that does pass through the site.

### Hosting

The site is static and hosted on GitHub Pages. Like most web hosts, GitHub automatically logs standard technical information for every visit (e.g. IP address, browser type, referring page) for security and operational purposes. This is handled by GitHub, not by me, under [GitHub's own Privacy Statement](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-general-privacy-statement).

### Embedded third-party content

A couple of pages embed content hosted by other providers. When your browser loads one of these embeds, your request goes directly to that provider, under their own privacy policy:

- **Map (Contact section, homepage):** map tiles are loaded from [OpenStreetMap](https://www.openstreetmap.org/copyright), which may log the request (including your IP address) on their tile servers.
- **Appointments page:** the booking calendar is a [Google Calendar](https://policies.google.com/privacy) embed, served directly by Google.

### Analytics and cookies

This site does not run any analytics or tracking scripts, and does not itself set cookies. (Embedded third-party content above may set its own cookies when loaded — that's outside my control and governed by the relevant provider's policy.)

### Contact information

The email address, phone number, and office address shown on this site are provided so you can reach me directly. Submitting a message via email or phone is voluntary, and any information you share that way is used only to respond to you.

### Questions

If you have questions about this policy or how any of the above works, contact me at [chaves@uma.es](mailto:chaves@uma.es).
